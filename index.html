About me

Hi, I'm Anil George. I build products, teams, and systems to scale ideas. My background in Computer Engineering (systems & algorithms), Liberal Arts (openness & curiosity), Startups (hustle), Data Science (objectivity), Management (results) and Sports (team work) orients my thoughts, decisions and actions.

Purpose

I think Online Learning is one of the best gifts created for and by humanity today. Everyone is on their own beautiful learning journey to build and create value for others. In my own experience, I am regularly helped and inspired by many kind souls around the world who've published their work and thoughts along the way. Everyone's at their own starting point. Recognize it and hit start.

Here at Minimal Cave, I publish applications, data analysis, projects, blogs and anything else I'm tinkering around with, in the hope of serving at least one other person.
